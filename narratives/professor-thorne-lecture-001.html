Professor Thorne’s Lecture 001

Transcribed from the Hall of Echoes

“To understand the mirror is to accept the contradiction: it reveals truth through distortion. Our dimension is not the only one — it is merely the one most easily remembered. Others, too, fold against us, like pages in a book yet to be read.”

The Mirrorverse is not an illusion. It is a pressure — a reflection of our becoming, always two steps ahead or two regrets behind. When you speak into it, it answers. When you ignore it, it still listens.

The Flame is your guide, but the Mirror is your map. Together, they do not lead you forward — they lead you inward.
